HOW TO ADD YOUR LOGO
====================

1. Save your logo image as:  logo.png
2. Place it in THIS folder:  public/logo.png
3. That's it! The app will automatically use it everywhere (header, footer, login page, 404 page, etc.)

LOGO TIPS:
- Use a PNG with transparent background for best results
- Recommended size: at least 200x200 pixels
- The app scales it automatically based on where it appears

After adding logo.png, redeploy to Vercel (just push to GitHub — Vercel will auto-deploy).
